For the “BDATASET”: Dimension: The dataset has a certain number of rows
(observations) and columns (variables). Range of Years: The dataset
covers a range of years, from the earliest year to the latest year
present in the original datasets. Key Variables: The key variables in
this dataset are year, month, president, snp_close, and
unemployment_rate. year and month are used as keys for merging,
president indicates the political party (GOP or DEM), snp_close

shows the unemployment rate for a specific year and month.

exploring the relationship between political events, stock market
performance, and unemployment over time.
